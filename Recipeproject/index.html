Delicious banana bread, and why you should make it:

An easy and moist Banana Bread Recipe that is loaded with bananas, tangy-sweet raisins, and toasted walnuts.
This is one of our favorite overripe banana recipes with hundreds of 5-star reviews.

This is my husband’s recipe which he has perfected over the years,
and he is known for his Banana Bread. He makes it frequently and it always disappears fast!
It is simple to make and our favorite way to use up overripe bananas so they never go to waste.
It’s super moist and makes a great breakfast on-the-go.

The Ingredients

  • very ripe bananas
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup walnuts

The Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Grease and flour a bread loaf pan
  3. Lightly roast walnuts on a skillet, continuously stirring so they won’t burn.
  4. Coarsely chop and cool to room temperature.
  5. In a mixing bowl, cream together 8 Tbsp softened butter and 3/4 cup sugar.
  6. Mash bananas with a fork until the consistency of chunky applesauce
  7. Add them to the batter along with 2 eggs, mixing until blended
  8. In a separate bowl, whisk together: 1 1/2 cups of flour, 1 tsp of baking soda and 1/2 tsp of salt then add to batter.
  9. Add 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract and mix in chopped walnuts and raisins, and Pour into prepared loaf pan
  10. Bake at 350˚F for 55-60 min or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let banana bread rest for 10 min before transferring to a wire rack to cool.
